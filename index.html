About Me

I am Sandhiya Manikandan — an entry-level Business Analyst based in Mississauga, Ontario. I bring a strong foundation in requirements elicitation, process documentation, and stakeholder collaboration, applying BABOK®-aligned techniques across both academic and professional engagements.

With over 15 years across travel management, operations, and financial technology, I offer a cross-industry perspective that enables me to quickly understand business context and translate complex needs into structured, actionable requirements.

I am currently completing a Postgraduate Certificate in Information Systems & Business Analysis at George Brown College (T405, Co-op), and am a proud ECBA® candidate through IIBA®, expected July/August 2026.

Passionate about financial technology and committed to contributing to cross-functional teams where analytical rigour and clear communication create measurable results.
